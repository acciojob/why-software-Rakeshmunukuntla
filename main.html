Why I want to become a developer?

High demand: Software developers are in high demand, and the tech skills gap is real. Good salary: Software developers are well-paid, and their salaries can increase if they develop their own software. Creative outlet: Coding can be a creative outlet, and developers are expected to come up with bright ideas to create and enhance new products. Problem-solving: Software development requires problem-solving abilities, and developers need to be able to solve even the most challenging problems. Flexibility: Developers can often work remotely or freelance, and they can work in many types of companies. Continuous learning: The technology industry moves quickly, so developers are constantly learning and advancing in their careers. Shaping the future: Developers are shaping the future, and they can make a difference. Attention to detail: Attention to detail is an essential skill for developers.
